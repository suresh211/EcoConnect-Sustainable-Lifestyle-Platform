User segments

The platform is designed primarily for eco-conscious individuals and local eco-friendly businesses.

Individuals / Eco-Conscious Users

Primary users who want to track their daily behaviour and lower their carbon footprint.

  • Log daily sustainable actions.
  • View personal carbon progress.
  • Join events, challenges and earn rewards.
Local Eco-Friendly Businesses

Verified businesses that offer sustainable products and services.

  • Create a business profile.
  • Upload sustainability proof for verification.
  • Offer promotions and rewards to local users.
